# Olist-Dashboard
Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard using the Brazilian Olist E-commerce dataset to analyze sales performance, customer distribution, product categories, payment methods, and customer review trends. The dashboard helps businesses understand customer behavior, identify top-performing product categories, and monitor overall revenue growth.
# Key Objectives
- Analyze overall business performance using KPIs
- Identify top-performing product categories
- Track monthly sales trends and revenue growth
- Understand customer distribution across states
- Analyze payment method preferences
- Build an interactive and user-friendly business dashboard
# Business Insights Generated
- Revenue was heavily driven by credit card transactions.
- Mid-year months showed stronger sales performance.
- Home decor and beauty categories had higher customer demand.
- Customer concentration was highest in southeastern Brazil.
